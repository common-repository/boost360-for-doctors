This plugin enables you to convert your Wordpress website into a secure online clinic. This will enable patients to book appointment directly from your website. This plugin will also enable you with a secure and compliant Video Consultation module for you to connect with your patients online. 


== Description ==

Convert your Wordpress website into an [Online Clinic](https://online-clinic.getboost360.com) or [watch video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UYjlbTnF-YE). 

* Manage your patient appointments directly using the Boost app (available on android, ios and web).
* Create and showcase your e-profile to patients
* List your services, add health tips and relevant updates for your patients
* Enable patients to book appointments for:
** Clinical consultation
** Online consultation
* Easy appointment scheduling & fee collection
** Send reminders
** Enable pay-Later
* Collect and manage patient Feedback on every appointment
* View and manage patient records associated with completed appointments
* E-prescriptions
* HIPAA compliant and encrypted data storage
* Social media management
* Multiple doctor support for a clinic


== Installation ==

How to install the plugin?

1. Install "Boost360 for Doctors" plugin and activate the plugin.
1. Go to Settings > Boost360 for Doctors
1. Follow the steps mentioned to enable Online Clinic functionalities
1. Ensure that in your Boost account you have your doctor's profile created & approved. This is to ensure that only valid doctors (with Medical License registered in India) are able to consult online.
